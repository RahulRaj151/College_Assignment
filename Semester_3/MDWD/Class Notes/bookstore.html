About the Bookworm Bookshop

Since 1933, The bookworm Bookshop has offered rare and hard-to-find titles for the discerning readers. The Bookworm offers:

  • Friendly, knowledgable and courteous help
  • Free coffee and juice for our customers
  • A well-lit reading room so you can "try before you buy"
  • Four friendly cats: Esmerelda, Catherine, Dulciena and Beatrice

Our hours are 10am to 9pm weekdays, noon to 7 on weekends.
