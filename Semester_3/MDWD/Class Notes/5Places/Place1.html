Bangalore

Bengaluru, also known as Bangalore, is the second largest city in India. It is the capital of Karnataka state. Bengaluru is a major economic hub and is known as the Silicon Valley of India due to its dominance in the IT sector. Many major tech companies like Infosys, Wipro, Microsoft etc have offices in Bengaluru. The city has a nice climate throughout the year which is neither too hot nor too cold. It has a cosmopolitan culture and people from all over India have made it their home. Bengaluru is well-connected via air, rail and road to other cities. It has a well-developed infrastructure in terms of housing, healthcare, education etc. The city has lively pubs, clubs and restaurants serving a variety of cuisines. Bengaluru is a major tourist attraction with places like Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Cubbon Park and Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace.
