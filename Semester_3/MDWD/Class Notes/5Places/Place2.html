Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the third largest and capital city of Telangana state. It was established in 1591 CE and has a rich cultural heritage. Hyderabad used to be the capital of the princely state of Hyderabad during the rule of Nizams. The city has a mixture of Mughal and indigenous architecture with buildings like Charminar, Golconda Fort and Qutub Shahi Tombs attracting tourists. Hyderabad is now a major IT hub generating about $35 billion in export revenue annually. Many pharma and biotech companies are based in Hyderabad. The city has a pleasant climate throughout the year and attracts migrants from various parts of India. Hyderabad is known for its unique culture and cuisine which is a blend of the Nizams' Arabian tastes and local Telugu flavors. Biryani is the most popular dish here besides other Mughlai dishes.
