Indore

Indore is the largest and most populous city of Madhya Pradesh. It is recognized as one of India's cleanest, greenest and most developed city and was awarded the title "India's Best City" in 2016. Indore has emerged as a major commercial center and is known as the financial capital of Madhya Pradesh. It has a strong base in industries like automobiles, electronics and food processing. The city has state of the art infrastructure and civic services. It is well-planned with wide roads and parks like Lal Bagh and Rajwada Palace attracting many visitors. Indore is also an educational hub with top universities, colleges and institutes. The residents belong to diverse communities and celebrate all major festivals enthusiastically. The famous Indore style chili peppers and poha are loved across India.
