Lucknow

Lucknow, the capital city of Uttar Pradesh is the fifth largest urban agglomeration in India. It was previously the capital of the Awadh region under the Nawabs. Lucknow has a very rich cultural heritage and is known for its tehzeeb or refined culture. The city has splendid monumental architecture like the Bara Imambara, Rumi Darwaza and Chota Imambara from the Nawabi era. Lucknow is known for its handicrafts especially chikankari embroidery and is famous for its cuisine. Lucknowi biryani and kebabs are considered among the finest in India. As the administrative capital of UP, Lucknow is a major educational hub with many colleges and universities. It has emerged as an important textile industry centre as well and is well connected by air, roads and rail to other Indian cities. Lucknow has a moderate climate throughout the year making it livable.
