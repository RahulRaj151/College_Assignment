Traffic Awareness

Traffic Rules to Follow:

  • Always wear a seatbelt while driving.
  • Obey speed limits posted on road signs.
  • Do not use a mobile phone while driving.
  • Always stop at red traffic lights.
  • Yield to pedestrians at crosswalks.
  • Use turn signals when changing lanes or turning.
  • Do not drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  • Keep a safe distance from the vehicle in front of you.
  • Always check mirrors before changing lanes.
  • Do not run yellow lights; stop if safe to do so.

Traffic Signals Information:
