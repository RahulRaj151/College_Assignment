Jamshedpur

  • Dimna lake
    • The Dimna lake is an ideal picnic spot and is situated 13km from Jamshedpur. It is an artificial lake in Mirzadih Panchayat of Patamda Block . Dimna lake is surrounded by variety of flora, and can be reached after a beautiful hilly drive. It is situated at the foot hill of Dalma hills. The interesting thing to note is that the whole of the city of Jamshedpur gets its water supply from this lake, which was built by Tata Steel
  • Jubilee Park

    • One of the biggest parks in the country, the Jubilee Park is spread on a sprawling campus of 237.75 acres. The park was inaugurated in the year 1956 including lush green landscape, an amusement park, skating ring and a horticulture garden which contains a wide variety of plant species.

  • Zoological park

    • Located inside the Jubilee Park, the Tata Steel Zoological Park is an unkempt zoo which houses wildlife in its natural habitat. The park gives a respite to people with long and tiring jobs in the city and makes them see and experience nature and wildlife in their most pristine form.

  • Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary

    • Established in the year 1975, the Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary is situated at an altitude of 3000 feet and is spread over an area of 193 sq km. An extremely wide variety of the rarest of the species of fauna can be found here, like tigers, leopards, elephants, barking deer etc.
