Welcome To the Dumber project Website

Dunbar School was completed in january 1918, for the purpose of eucating Tucson's African-American students. The School was named after Paul Laurence Dunbar, a Renowed African-American Poet. African-American Children in first through ninth grades attended Dumbar until 1951, When de jure sergation was eliminated form the school systemof Arizona. When segregation in Arizona was eliminated, Dunbar School become the non-Segregated John Spring Junoir High School, and Continued as such until 1978 when the School was closed permanently.
